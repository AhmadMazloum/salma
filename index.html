About Me

I am a 19 years old Aspiring Artist/ Illustrator based in Alexandria,Egypt. Currently studying at Faculty of Fine arts (Decor department) with a passion for visual storytelling through mainly ordinary black pens -finding magic in the ordinary- to create detailed illustrations. “Sylm” is what started as place to simply share these illustrations with other people, but after multiple feedback ranging from friends, strangers, to a local newspaper & even potential customers, it kept growing - and still is growing- into a sideline project to my main professional outlets.
